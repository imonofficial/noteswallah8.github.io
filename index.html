Effective Date: October 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NotesWallah, a student-centric educational platform developed and maintained by a consortium of dedicated scholars. This Privacy Policy delineates our protocols regarding the collection, utilization, and safeguarding of information when you engage with our digital ecosystem.

Our mission is to democratize education through the provision of freely accessible academic resources, while maintaining the highest standards of data protection and user privacy.

2. Intellectual Property Framework

All proprietary tools, pedagogical materials, and digital assets disseminated through this platform constitute original intellectual property developed exclusively for non-commercial educational utilization.

  • Unauthorized reproduction, modification, distribution, or commercial exploitation of our resources is expressly prohibited without prior written authorization.
  • Infringements of our intellectual property rights will result in immediate revocation of access privileges and may incur legal repercussions, including copyright enforcement actions.
  • Our organization maintains complete autonomy and is not institutionally affiliated with any educational establishment or corporate entity.
  • Content is licensed for personal educational utilization under the principles of open knowledge sharing and academic integrity.

3. Information Collection Protocols

In accordance with our security framework, we may collect limited personal identifiers for verification and protective measures:

  • Identifiable Information: Full name as provided by the user
  • Contact Data: Valid email address for communication and verification purposes

Data Minimization Principle: We adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting only information essential for platform security and user verification. No automated data harvesting occurs without explicit user consent.

4. Purpose Specification

Collected information serves exclusively the following legitimate purposes:

  • Security Infrastructure: Protection of our digital assets, including Google Drive repositories and educational tools
  • Access Control: Prevention of unauthorized system infiltration and cyber threats
  • User Authentication: Verification of legitimate student status and platform membership

We explicitly refrain from user tracking, behavioral monitoring, or any form of commercial data exploitation.

5. Data Protection Mechanisms

Our organization implements industry-standard security protocols to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of user data. All collected information is stored within encrypted environments and undergoes scheduled secure deletion once its designated purpose has been fulfilled.

We maintain rigorous security measures for all integrated third-party systems, including Google Drive infrastructure, to prevent unauthorized access attempts.

6. Non-Profit Status Declaration

NotesWallah operates as a non-commercial educational initiative managed exclusively by students. Our platform generates no revenue, displays no advertisements, and imposes no financial obligations on users. All resources are provided gratuitously in the spirit of academic collaboration.

7. Third-Party Integrations

Our platform may incorporate links to external digital environments (e.g., Google Drive). We expressly disclaim responsibility for the privacy practices or content governance of these external platforms. Users are advised to review the respective privacy policies of these third-party services prior to engagement.

8. Policy Modification Protocol

This Privacy Policy constitutes a dynamic document that may undergo periodic revisions to reflect evolving legal requirements and platform enhancements. Substantive amendments will be communicated through platform notifications and updated effective dates. Continued utilization of our services following policy modifications constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
